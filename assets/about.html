Ankidroid:
Flashcards for Android

Ankidroid can read .anki files produced by the desktop Anki software.

Warning: Ankidroid modifies .anki files in a way that is not compatible with the Anki desktop application. Do not copy .anki files from the SD card back to your computer.

You can report bugs here or discuss on the forum.

Ankidroid is open source software, and everyone is encouraged to get the source and build on it :-)
